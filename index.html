Sound Board Settings

Power Up

When you are preparing for a service, use these steps to assure all devices you will need have power.

  • Power on the surge protector on the floor under the board.
    This powers the sound board, wireless receivers, USB recorder, CD and tape player.
  • Check for lights on
    • the surge protector
    • the both amplifiers to the right of the surge protector
    • the CD player
    • the USB recorder
    • the all wireless receivers on the wire rack above the amplifiers
  • Make sure all channels are muted
  • Master L is at level 0
  • Master R is at level -30
  • Mon 1 is at level 0
  • Mon2 is at level 0
  • Put the USB drive in the USB recorder
    • Press the record button to prepare recording
    • The display should change to "Preparing"
    • After preparing the record light should start blinking
      This means the recorder will begin recording when the "record" button is pressed
    • When the record button is pressed the display light should be on solid and the display counter should increment each second to show it is recording
    • To stop the recording
      • Press the stop button on the recorder
      • Wait for "Finalizing" to disappear from the display
      • Remove the USB drive
      • Place the USB drive in the PC for processing

Power Down

When the service ends, follow these steps to properly power down the sound equipment

  • Make sure the USB recording has ended
    • The record light should be off - press the stop button if it is still recording
    • Make sure the display does not show "Finalizing" - if so, wait until "Finalizing" disappears from the display
    • Remove the USB drive from the recorder
    • Place the USB drive in the PC for processing
  • Make sure all channels are muted
  • Master L is at level 0
  • Master R is at level -30
  • Mon 1 is at level 0
  • Mon2 is at level 0
  • Power off the surge protector on the floor under the board

Playing a CD

CDs are played in the Denon device on your right. Insert the disc. If it starts playing, press the pause button.

  • Make sure the A/B switch between the PC and sound board is in the "B" position
  • Select the track you want to play using the track forward/backward buttons (you can use the remote or the front panel)
  • Channel 8? unmuted - use slider to adjust volume (this is the music side of a split-track CD)
  • If the CD is split-track, leave track 7? muted.
  • Unmute the channels for each microphone that will be used
  • Mute all other channels
  • Master L is at level 0
  • Master R is at level 0
  • Mon 1 is at level 0
  • Mon2 is at level 0
  • After playing the song(s) move Mon2 back to level -30

Pastor is preaching

When the pastor is preaching here are the sound board settings.

  • Channel 2 unmuted - use slider to adjust volume
  • All other channels muted
  • Master L is at level 0
  • Master R is at level -30
  • Mon 1 is at level 0
  • Mon2 is at level 0
